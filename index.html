Rob Denford - Head Coach

Rob is the current Tennis Professional at Elm Park Golf & Sports Club and is responsible for organising and running the junior and adult programs, with the help of fellow coach Victoria Gaidau. Previously, Rob was head coach in Sandyroad Tennis & Squash Club, for 10 years up to June 2017. With a previous career in IT, he has a keen interest in using Video Analysis to assist him in coaching players of all levels. Rob has helped coach several players to Junior National Titles in various age groups and has achieved the status of “ITF iCoach Expert“.

Jean Rourke - Beginner coach

Jean is the current beginner/intermediate coach at the various clubs in the leinster reigion of ireland and has great skills for teaching tennis in fun set ups. Teaching over 12 years Jean has a great track record of teaching students to make progress with there repective goals. Specialising in new players introduction to the sport jean finds helpful and most importantly fun ways to make tennis learning enjoyable for all age groups and catagories.
